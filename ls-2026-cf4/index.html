★ Glossary
CTR (Click-Through Rate)
The percentage of people who see your listing or ad and click on it.
CPC (Cost Per Click)
What you pay each time someone clicks your ad.
CPL (Cost Per Lead)
What you pay, on average, for each phone call or form submission.
CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
What it costs to win one paying customer.
ROAS (Return On Ad Spend)
Revenue earned for every dollar spent on ads.
GBP (Google Business Profile)
Your free business listing on Google Maps and Search.
NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
Your business identity info — must match exactly everywhere online.
Map Pack
The top 3 local business results shown on a Google Maps search.
LSA (Local Services Ads)
Google's pay-per-lead ad program shown above all other results.
Schema Markup
Hidden code that helps Google show your star rating and details directly in search results.
GTM (Google Tag Manager)
A tool that tracks what visitors do on your website.
GA4 (Google Analytics 4)
Google's website traffic and conversion tracking platform.
LTV (Lifetime Value)
The total revenue one customer brings over time.
RSA (Responsive Search Ad)
A Google Ads format that mixes headlines and descriptions automatically.
Review Velocity
How many new reviews you earn per month.
Citation
A mention of your business name, address, and phone number on another website or directory.
