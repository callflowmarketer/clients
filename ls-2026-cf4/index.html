Reference

Glossary of Terms

CTR — Click-Through Rate: the percentage of people who click your listing after seeing it.
CPC — Cost Per Click: what you pay each time someone clicks a Google Ad.
CPL — Cost Per Lead: what you pay for each verified phone call or message.
CPA — Cost Per Acquisition: total ad spend divided by the number of jobs booked.
ROAS — Return On Ad Spend: revenue generated for every dollar spent on ads.
GBP — Google Business Profile: your free business listing on Google Maps and Search.
NAP — Name, Address, Phone: your business details, which must match exactly everywhere online.
Map Pack — the 3-listing map box that appears at the top of local Google searches.
LSA — Local Services Ads: pay-per-lead ads shown above the Map Pack, with a "Google Guaranteed" badge.
Schema Markup — hidden code on your website that helps Google display rich details like your rating.
GTM — Google Tag Manager: a tool that installs tracking code on your website.
GA4 — Google Analytics 4: reports on where your website visitors and calls come from.
LTV — Lifetime Value: the total revenue an average customer generates over time.
RSA — Responsive Search Ad: a Google Ad format that mixes headlines and descriptions automatically.
Review Velocity — how many new Google reviews you earn per month.
Citation — any online directory listing of your business name, address, and phone number.
