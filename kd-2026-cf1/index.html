Glossary
Not sure what a term means? Everything defined in plain English.
Citation
Any online listing that mentions your business Name, Address, and Phone number — used by Google to verify your location and improve your local ranking.
CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
How much it costs to gain one paying patient from your ads. If you spend CAD $600 on ads and get 4 new patients, your CPA is CAD $150.
CPC (Cost Per Click)
How much you pay each time someone clicks your Google Ad. Dental keywords in Saskatoon average CAD $8–12 per click.
CPL (Cost Per Lead)
Total ad spend divided by the number of leads (calls or form fills) you received. Lower is better.
CTR (Click-Through Rate)
The percentage of people who saw your listing or ad and clicked on it. A listing at Position #1 in the Map Pack gets roughly 42% of all clicks.
GA4 (Google Analytics 4)
Google's website analytics platform that tracks who visits your site, where they came from, and what they do — essential for measuring ad ROI.
GBP (Google Business Profile)
Your free Google listing that appears in Maps and local search results when people search "dentist Saskatoon." This is the single most important local SEO asset you have.
GTM (Google Tag Manager)
A tool that lets you add tracking codes to your website without editing the site's code directly. Used to measure phone calls, form submissions, and ad conversions.
LSA (Local Services Ads)
Google ads that appear above all regular search ads with a "Google Verified" badge. You pay per lead, not per click — and dental clinics qualify in Saskatchewan.
LTV (Lifetime Value)
The total revenue a single patient is expected to generate over their relationship with your clinic. A patient who visits twice yearly for 5 years at CAD $300/visit = CAD $3,000 LTV minimum.
Map Pack
The box of 3 local business listings shown at the top of Google search results, with a map. Being in the top 3 is the single biggest driver of new patient calls from search.
NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
The three pieces of business contact information that must be identical everywhere online. Even minor differences (like "Blvd" vs "Boulevard") can hurt your local ranking.
Review Velocity
The average number of new reviews your business receives per month. Higher velocity signals an active, trusted business to Google. Your current rate is 0.9/month; target is 10–15/month.
ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
Revenue earned for every dollar spent on ads. A 19× ROAS means for every CAD $1 you spend on Google Ads, you earn CAD $19 back in patient revenue.
RSA (Responsive Search Ad)
A Google ad format where you provide multiple headlines and descriptions, and Google automatically tests combinations to find which ones get the most calls and clicks.
Schema Markup
Hidden code added to your website that helps Google understand what your business does. MedicalBusiness schema helps dental clinics rank higher in health-related searches.
