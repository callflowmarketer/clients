Glossary of Terms

CTR (Click-Through Rate)
The percentage of people who see your ad or listing and actually click on it.
CPC (Cost Per Click)
How much you pay each time someone clicks your Google Ad. Locksmith CPCs in Winnipeg: CAD $8–$28.
CPL (Cost Per Lead)
How much you spend in ads to get one phone call or inquiry from a potential customer.
CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
Total cost to acquire one paying customer — ad spend divided by jobs won.
ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
Revenue earned for every dollar spent on ads. 3× ROAS = $3 earned per $1 spent.
GBP (Google Business Profile)
Your free Google listing that appears in Maps and the "Map Pack" search results.
NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
Your business contact details — must be identical across every website and directory.
Map Pack
The 3 local businesses shown on a map at the top of Google results. Position #1 gets 42% of clicks.
LSA (Local Services Ads)
Google ads appearing above everything with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. Pay per verified lead call only.
Schema Markup
Hidden code telling Google exactly what your business does — boosts local ranking signals.
GTM (Google Tag Manager)
Tool to install tracking codes on your website without editing code manually.
GA4 (Google Analytics 4)
Google's analytics platform — shows where visitors come from and which ads generate calls.
LTV (Lifetime Value)
Total revenue one customer generates — locksmith customers return for rekeying, new keys, etc.
RSA (Responsive Search Ad)
Google's ad format where you provide headlines and descriptions; Google mixes them for best performance.
Review Velocity
The rate of new Google reviews per month — higher velocity improves Map Pack ranking.
Citation
Any online mention of your business name, address, and phone on a directory — builds local authority.
