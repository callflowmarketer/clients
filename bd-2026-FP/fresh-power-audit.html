Reference

Glossary

Terms used in this audit — plain language, no jargon.

GBP
Google Business Profile. The listing that appears on Google Maps and Search when someone searches for your service. It includes your name, rating, photos, hours, and address.
Map Pack
The top 3 local business results shown on Google Maps for a search query. Appearing here generates 42% of all clicks — the most valuable real estate in local search.
Review Velocity
How many new reviews your profile receives per month. Google rewards consistent, recent reviews over old bulk reviews. Target: 10+ per month.
CTR
Click-Through Rate. The percentage of people who click your listing after seeing it. Map Pack #1 gets ~42% CTR. Positions 4+ get under 5%.
Schema Markup
Hidden code on your website that tells Google exactly what your business does, where you serve, and what services you offer — improving ranking accuracy.
GTM / GA4
Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics 4. Tools that track how visitors use your website — which buttons they click, which pages they visit, and which actions lead to calls.
Citation
Any online listing of your business Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP). Consistent citations on 20+ directories signal legitimacy to Google and improve Map Pack ranking.
Service-Area Page
A dedicated page on your website targeting a specific neighbourhood (e.g. "AC Installation Banani"). Tells Google you serve that area even if your office isn't there.
CallFlow™
Alauddin's digital growth system for service businesses. Combines GBP optimisation, conversion-focused landing pages, review systems, and ad management to drive inbound calls.
