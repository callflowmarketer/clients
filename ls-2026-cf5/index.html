Glossary of Terms

CTRClick-Through Rate — the percentage of people who see your listing and click it.
CPCCost Per Click — what you pay each time someone clicks your ad.
CPLCost Per Lead — what you pay for each verified phone call or form submission.
CPACost Per Acquisition — total cost to win one paying customer.
ROASReturn On Ad Spend — revenue generated for every dollar spent on ads.
GBPGoogle Business Profile — your free business listing on Google Maps and Search.
NAPName, Address, Phone — must match exactly everywhere online.
Map PackThe map + 3 business listings shown at the top of local Google searches.
LSALocal Services Ads — Google's pay-per-lead ads with a Google Guaranteed badge, shown above everything else.
Schema MarkupCode added to a website that helps Google understand and display your business info.
GTMGoogle Tag Manager — a tool for installing tracking code without editing the site directly.
GA4Google Analytics 4 — tracks where your website visitors and leads come from.
LTVLifetime Value — total revenue a customer generates over the whole relationship.
RSAResponsive Search Ad — a Google Ads format that mixes headlines and descriptions automatically.
Review VelocityHow many new reviews you earn per month — a key ranking and trust signal.
CitationAny online listing of your business name, address, and phone number — builds local trust with Google.
